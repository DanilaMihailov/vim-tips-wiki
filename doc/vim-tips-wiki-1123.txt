TIP 1123 HAS BEEN REMOVED                 *vtw-1123-tip-1123-has-been-removed*

which have been superseded, or which are no longer useful.

The original tip was at vim.org which may be in this archive Tip#1123 
[http://web.archive.org/web/www.vim.org/tips/tip.php?tip_id=1123].
