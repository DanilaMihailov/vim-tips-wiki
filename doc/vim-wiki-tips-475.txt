==============================================================================

Use this vmap to enclose a block with fold markers:

>
    vmap <Leader>fold mz:<Esc>'<O// {{{<Esc>'>o// }}}<Esc>`z?{{{<CR>A<Space>
<

COMMENTS  *vwt-475-comments*

Prefer the zf command, in combination with foldmethod=marker. See :help |zf|.
