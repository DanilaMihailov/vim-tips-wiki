==============================================================================

Create and use $TMP/tags for local source dir (which may be unwriteable). 
Unix users can change c:/tmp to ~/tmp/ (I use $TMP with sh).

>
    :map <c-k>t :!(cd %:p:h;ctags -o c:/tmp/tags -R .)&<CR>:set tags^=c:/tmp/tags<CR>
<
